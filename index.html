Speakers

Please give a warm welcome to the conference speakers. They come from a variety of backgrounds and they’re all keen to share their knowledge and experience with you.

Michael Essek

Top UK Mercher

Michael Essek is a designer and illustrator with over 5 years experience in the online T-Shirt licensing industry.

Michael helps artists and designers better understand the print-on-demand and licensing business, so they can generate better ideas, create better designs and make a sustainable income from their work.

Jacob Topping

Print On Demand expert and bestselling author

Jacob Topping is an internationally sought after public speaker and author of the book: Merch and the World of Print On Demand – which has sold over 3000+ copies to online sellers looking to learn the exact step by step how to’s of launching White Label and Private Label products via Print On Demand.

Selling online since at least 1999, Jacob has sold items across multiple domains and sells across multiple Marketplaces including: Amazon (FBA, FBM), Merch By Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, Ebay, and over 25+ additional online marketplaces. Living near Canada’s capital city of Ottawa, Jacob is the father of 3 young kids (8-9-10), and husband to Tara Topping; Jacob Travels the world teaching others to sell more and earn more profit.

Mark Shaggy

Digital Entrepreneur - POD Enthusiast and Tier 32k Mercher

Mark Czuprynski

Online he's better known as Mark Shaggy as his Polish surname is hard to pronounce and hard to remember. He self-thought everything he knows about making money online, and he's a big fan of the digital nomad lifestyle.

Thanks to Merch by Amazon and POD business, he's been able to quit his full-time job in a factory. He's the example that if you want something hard enough and work on it long enough, you can achieve it.

Nicholas Chard

Intellectual Property Office Representative

Nicholas has worked for the IPO for 5 years. He has worked in various departments of the Office and is experienced in delivering and advising on all aspects of Intellectual Property. He is relatively new to the Outreach team and is excited to start traveling the UK delivering the IP message to SME’s, Stakeholders and promoting our online tools.

Mark Horley

Amazon Tax Specialist @ Passive Tax Accountants

Mark is an Amazon Tax & VAT Specialist Accountant who provides compliance services for his clients all over the UK and Europe from the offices of PassiveTAX in Nottingham which are a digital accountancy service provider.

Joe Compaan

Merch By Amazon Creator

Joe is primarily an Amazon FBA seller, selling physical products on the Amazon UK marketplace. He created his Merch by Amazon account about 18 months ago, and is currently Tier 1000. Joe likes to help others begin their own Merch journey by providing tips and advice on design research and how to get out of the lower tiers.

Besides this, Joe enjoys learning more about making money online and is an avid gamer with his own streaming channel on Twitch.

Ben Chinnock

KDP Creator

Ben Chinnock, a recent university graduate from Milton Keynes, started his KDP (Kindle Direct Publishing) low content journey a little less than a year ago, and has sold 4000+ books to date, with 1500+ books published, earning him over £1000 p/m every month since November.

Ben will be talking about everything KDP during this event, from an insight into the business itself, and getting started with your very own account, to tips and tricks into sourcing niches and building a successful and profitable publishing business.
